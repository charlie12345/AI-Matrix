Fusion sweep recap

Native 262k winner plus a real 524k YaRN keep

This page is for the exact tested file NVIDIA-Nemotron-3-Nano-30B-A3B-MXFP4_MOE.gguf. It is not the separate Omni/Reasoning GGUF line. In the main matrix, the server ran with thinking off, and this model behaved more like the stronger Qwen 3.6 MXFP4 MoE lanes than the older Nemotron IQ4/NVFP4 paths. It held roughly 26 tok/s decode from 32k through native 262k, then stayed in the same band through 393k and 524k YaRN with the no-cache long-context setup.

Hidden-answer suite

Repeated Logic Misses

  • semantic_interference: common failure. The model kept saying the feather would hit the ceiling first instead of the bowling ball.
  • myopic_planning: weaker stock lanes missed that the meeting schedule is impossible. Stronger turbo3 / q8_0 lanes recovered this.
  • vowels_i_count: common failure. It kept reporting the distinct vowels correctly, but overcounted the letter i as 3.
  • sally_anne: passed on the stronger lanes.
  • vibepass_bugfix: passed on the stronger lanes.
